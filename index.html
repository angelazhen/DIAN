Frequently Asked Questions

Our roses are hand-picked from pristine gardens in Yunnan, China, at an altitude of 2000 meters. This high-altitude environment allows the roses to develop their most intense flavor and aromatic properties, making them perfect for our premium pastries.

DIAN pastries are best enjoyed at room temperature. For an enhanced experience, pair them with a cup of tea or coffee. They make perfect additions to afternoon tea, breakfast spreads, or as elegant dessert options for special occasions.

Store the pastry in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and strong odors. Keep it sealed in its original packaging to preserve the flaky crust and the aroma of the rose filling. The shelf life is 120 days from the production date when stored properly.
