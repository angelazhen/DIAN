# DIAN

## Premium Handmade Rose Petal Pastries

DIAN is a premium artisanal pastry brand specializing in 100% handmade rose petal pastries, bringing the authentic flavors of Yunnan, China to New York City. Our pastries are crafted using hand-picked roses from pristine gardens at 2000 meters altitude, where the high-altitude environment allows the roses to develop their most intense flavor and aromatic properties.
